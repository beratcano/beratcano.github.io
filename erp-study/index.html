The 3 axes of the course

Every week maps onto one of these. Keep the big picture in mind.

① Processes

End-to-end flows that cross departments: Order-to-Cash, Procure-to-Pay, Plan-to-Produce, Hire-to-Retire. Think process, not department.

② Data

Master data (stable: customer, vendor, material, accounts) vs transactional data (daily: orders, invoices, movements). Garbage in → garbage out.

③ Implementation

Selection, project, change & risk. Most ERP value — and most ERP failure — lives here, not in the software itself.
