Why I built this

My girlfriend studies landscape architecture. She needed a way to visualize how plant colors change throughout the year. So I built this for her.

What it does

Interactive circular visualization of seasonal plant colors throughout the year. Generates circular charts showing leaf color transitions across seasons. Each plant gets its own color wheel showing how it looks month by month.

Built with

  • Vanilla JavaScript
  • SVG
  • Data Visualization

What I learned

SVG manipulation, working with color data, and building something useful for someone you care about.
